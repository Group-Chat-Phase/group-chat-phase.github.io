  <h1>About</h1>
  <p>Group Chat Phase is a periodical thematic publication showcasing a mish-mash of creative projects. Each issue has a theme, chosen by the editors, which acts as a nexus loosely tying the various projects together. While the contents of the first edition were entirely the product of your dearest editors' belabored imagination, the idea behind Group Chat Phase is to open the publication to anybody who would like to share the progeny of their creative mind. If you would be interested in collaborating with us, feel free to contact one of the editors!
</p>
